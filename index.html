workload, and priorities.

increased collaboration both
internally across groups and
externally with out stakeholders.

Populations are cross cutting and are
at the center of the projects.

management focus on collaborating
across groups.

projects in a variety of clusters.

impactful products with a country focus
and person centered approach.
